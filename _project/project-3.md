**Fives European Combustion Centre, Italy — Sep 2020 to May 2022**

Research and development of **low-NOx and ultra-low-NOx burners**, covering the full test lifecycle: planning of testing activities, risk analysis and HAZOP studies, and combustion testing in line with safety procedures and functional specifications.

- Managed burners and furnaces and conducted combustion tests with various fuels, including **steam dilution** and **CO₂ injection**, proposing burner optimisations.
- Calibrated gas analysers and performed exhaust-gas analysis to evaluate **emissions and combustion efficiency**.
- Prepared combustion sheets, operating and validation procedures, process calculations, test reports, and concept notes.
- Supervised and coordinated with suppliers and delivered basic safety-induction and combustion training.
