*Manuscript in preparation (2026).*

This study experimentally and numerically characterizes OH\* radical chemiluminescence in spherically expanding premixed H₂–air flames. By examining the relationship between the OH\* signal and the heat-release rate, the work validates OH\* as a reliable, non-intrusive flame-front marker for hydrogen combustion and identifies the chemical pathways governing OH\* formation under varying conditions.

**Authors:** K. G. Subramanya, G. Dayma, C. Chauveau, F. Halter.
