*Manuscript in preparation (2026).*

This work characterizes the spatial and spectral signatures of premixed H₂–air flames through optical diagnostics, contributing to the development of advanced non-intrusive sensing techniques for hydrogen combustion.

**Authors:** K. G. Subramanya, S.-Y. Lee, V. Lago, C. Chauveau, F. Halter.
