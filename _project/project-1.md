**Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering, India — Jun 2010 to Jun 2014 (B.E. Thesis)**

The objective of this undergraduate research was to evaluate the feasibility of converting selected components of **municipal solid waste (MSW)** into **biogas** via anaerobic fermentation using fungi.

Using a laboratory-scale experimental setup, I studied the decomposition and fermentation behaviour of the waste fractions and characterised the **biogas-production kinetics** of the anaerobic digestion process.
